Dropping Module with 'W' Grade

22 February 2016. Tuesday 5:09 PM

It is the start of Recess Week! Please take this time to catch up and revise for Mid-Terms! But do also take care of your own health, heave plenty of trest and drink plenty of water! :D

Please be reminded of the following:

Last day of Recess Week: 28 Feb 16, Sunday.
If you wish to drop the module, please do so before 28 feb 16, to avoid getting the Fail Grade.
For a module dropped with an 'F' or 'CU' grade, you will not be able to apply the S/U option during the S/U declaration exercise.

To drop a module:
1. Login to CORS: http://www.nus.edu.sg/cors/
2. Select 'Module Management' on the left panel.
3. Check the checkbox for the module you wish to sop.
4. Click the 'Drop' button
